**Join us at Petone Community Library for a hands-on display of historical items from the collections of the Library and the Petone Settlers Museum. You’ll get to handle a range of items about the history of Lower Hutt, including postcards, photographs, posters, maps and objects.** 

No need to book, just wander in between 10am and 3pm on Saturday 2 November. The Hutt City Council’s Archive was also be represented.

<br>

**No booking is required.**

**Location:** Petone Community Library, 7 Britannia Street

**Cost:** Free

**Email Contact:** Petone.library@huttcity.govt.nz

**Telephone Contact:** 04 568 6523
